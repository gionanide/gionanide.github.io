Projects

A complete listing of research and engineering projects, mostly in medical imaging and applied deep learning.

UNiCA: deep learning for cancer treatment imaging

EU4H · University of Ioannina · 2025 to present

PhD work as part of the Unified Network for International Cancer Advancement, an EU consortium developing imaging methodologies to support cancer treatment decisions across collaborating institutions.

Endpoint-aware risk triage for medical image segmentation

Currently under revision · Target: Medical Image Analysis

A lightweight region-aware scoring layer that reweights segmentation predictions by clinical risk. Evaluated on histopathology and polyp segmentation datasets. The goal is to surface the regions where a missed prediction matters most, rather than treating every pixel equally.

FDA-cleared X-ray segmentation for pre-op templating

Ortho AI · 2023 to present

Lead the R&D of the segmentation models powering an automated radiological image-processing product for hip, spine, and knee templating. Cleared by the FDA under 510(k) K241696. Built the bootstrap-based statistical validation pipeline for regulatory reporting and the post-market monitoring stack for data drift and retraining triggers.

Power analysis for FDA-aligned model monitoring

Ortho AI · 2024 · regulatory deliverable

Companion deliverable to the Ortho AI templating product, supporting its FDA-aligned monitoring requirements.

DICE-Net: Convolution-Transformer for Alzheimer detection in EEG

IEEE Access · 2023

A hybrid convolution-transformer architecture for Alzheimer's disease detection from EEG signals. Combines local feature extraction with longer-range attention over the temporal axis. Co-authored with the University of Ioannina medical informatics group.

Mapping vulnerability descriptions to adversary techniques

IEEE SecDev · 2022

NLP-based tool that ingests vulnerability descriptions and predicts which MITRE ATT&CK adversary techniques they enable. Adopted by HivePro to help companies prioritize cybersecurity planning. Investigated the role of text augmentation in low-data regimes for this task.
